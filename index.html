Krishna

He is the god of protection, compassion, tenderness, and love; and is widely revered among Hindu divinities.

Shiva

Shiva is a prominent god of Hinduism. His name means "Auspicious One," and he is a member of the trinity of supreme gods alongside Brahma

Hanuman

Hanuman is a very powerful and strong god. He finds an important place in the Ramayana. He is a devotee of Rama, a form (avatar) of Lord Vishnu, a god of Hindus.
